<Update label="January 15, 2026" description="v1.0" tags={["New release"]}>
  ## Frammerce 1.0 - Live on the Framer Marketplace

  The first public release of Frammerce. Connect a Shopify store to the Framer canvas, drag any of 21 commerce components onto the artboard, and ship a real storefront with no theme, no Liquid, and no backend.

  - **21 production-ready components** - 7 product blocks (Gallery, Price, Variant Picker, Quantity, Stock, Add to Cart, Buy Now) and 14 cart blocks (Drawer, List, Row, Line components, Subtotal, Checkout, etc.)
  - **One-click Shopify connect** - paste your `.myshopify.com` domain + Storefront access token and you're live
  - **Framer CMS sync** - pull every Shopify product into a Framer managed collection
  - **Auto-installed cart runtime** - one cart state shared across every page on the published site
  - **Sync Settings** - toggle compare-at price, vendor, tags, subscription support on/off per project

  [Quickstart](/quickstart)
</Update>
